Introduction

Almost all the business is dealing with supply chain. For example, pancake, wheat bread or coffee all those stuff you eat in this morning need to went through the supply chain and come to your house from the world. If there anything needs to assemble and move to one place to the other, it requires supply chain. The following is definitions form articles: â€œSupply chains are a set of three or more entities (organizations or individuals) directly involved in the upstream and downstream flows of products, services, finances, and/or information from a source to a customer" ([41] Mentzer et al, 2001, p. 3). And "supply chain management (SCM) is the integration of these activities through improved supply chain relationships, to achieve a sustainable competitive advantage" ([20] Handfield and Nichols, 1999, p. 2). The purpose of this paper is to present research that gives the students who are new to the field of Supply Chain Management and help them to understand what are Supply Chain Management and the path to succeed in this field. In addition, the paper provides a supply chain operating model that give audience a clear view of how supply chain process looks like. The paper is contains three major parts:

  • Discuss the background, traditional view, people in the field and the future of the supply chain management.
  • College education
  • Career path
