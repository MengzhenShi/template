Analysis of Artifact

Annie Leonard is an American proponent of sustainability and critic of excessive consumerism; she first realized that people in the U.S. are over-consuming which is causing a huge impact to the society. In order to call attention to the society, Annie Leonard made a video call the “Story Of The Stuff”. In this 20-minutes long video, Annie Leonard is being an aside speaker who talks to the audience with bunch of cartoons; and she shares concrete steps for tell us that how people and government should do in order to bring the sustainability, community health, and economic justice. Though the video, Leonard’s compassion and humor speaking makes audience quickly understand how stuff comes from the extraction and goes to the disposal; and also let audience realized the impacts that related to this process. The social issue, over-consuming, that Leonard mentioned is the core problem of the current society, and she use the video to show audiences why this in happening and how to solve it.
