College is not only a place to study, but is also a place to building the network circle. “There is no better time than now to strengthen or build your network. A solid network will serve you well for years to come and will be an additional tool in your toolbox especially during these times of economic uncertainty.” (Kathy, 2009) In the other words, networking is tool to help students to reach success in their career, especially, in the field of supply chain because it requires a huge networking web. How to build network circle in the school? Student should participate activities in the school or join a club that is related to their major, so that students could improve the teamwork ability by work with others in the activities. Academic student help student to gain the knowledge and the outside class help student understanding of the planning, organization and risk management and also gain the social experiences.

After received a high level education from college, finding a suitable job is most of graduates concerned about. Supply Chain Management involving both field of practitioners and researchers. Different job has different value to support the supply chain keep running.

To pick a suitable job is depends on what type of job that student preferred. For example, if a student wish to be an employee who connect with suppliers, tracking the PO and analyze cost of logistics to lower the cost, So that the position of supply chain manager will suit this student. If the student would like to be an employees who is like to planning and scheduling, so that the position of supply chain supervisor will suit the student. However, there has a principle for picking a job, which is No matter what position that people picked, the career path will always be comfort as long as the job is suitable.

Position in a company is one aspect that employees care about, and the other aspect is the salary. According to the Logistics Management 29th Annual Salary Survey, the average salary for supply chain and logistic professionals are increasing compare to the passed year.

As a member of supply chain management, join the APICS will be the best choice to help you to success in your career.

APICS (The Association for Operations Management) is the finest association of supply chain management in the world. As a APICS member, APICS will provides professional training in order to help each members to improve their performance in their career. The comprehensive suite of educational resources provides by APICS is in including (web):

  • Education, training, industry publications and research, and world-renowned certification programs
  • Opportunities for career development, networking, and best practice sharing
  • Local and global membership and affiliation opportunities worldwide

APICS is also provides APICS CPIM Designation. This program provides supply chain company employees an ability to understand evaluate production and inventory activities within a company’s global operations. What’ more, the APICS certification is a tool that helps employee to have a better position in their career path because this certification is give to people who has passing the high level examination called CPIM exams and also with high level professional abilities.

In the interview with Mr. Chen, the manager of a logistics company called Chaozhou Westside Logistics Company, he told a story of how he started his company in 1995. He was a car driver running for a logistics company in a small town. Basically, he was delivering cargos from one place to another. As time passes, he started to realize that there is some way to lower his delivery costs, so he jumped out and established his own company.

Knowledge that student learned from school such as the process of supply chain, analysis the input/output is not enough for support them to start a business. To start a new firm they must have years of experiences of work and also have a clear view of the supply chain market. And the leader must lead the company to keep up with step of time. “Companies are beginning to integrate their online and bricks-and-mortar businesses, Caplice says, resulting in many more options for purchasing and delivering products. You can order a product on a smartphone and pick it up at the store, or you can see something you like at the store, place an order for it, and have it delivered at home.”(Eric. 2014) In the other words, the technology field is updating every single day, so does the field of supply chain. A supply chain manager cannot stabilize one single process to running the business, as a leader in the field of supply chain must be creative.
